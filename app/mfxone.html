MortgageFlexONE

MortgageFlexONE is an enterprise-level loan origination system that offers the functionality you need regardless of the size of your business. It is designed to grow with your business.

We keep it simple by charging a fee per closed loan and a low monthly technology fee for access to the system. The number of users is unlimited. Your volumes can go up or down without impacting your cost per closed loan. That's it!

More Efficient

The MortgageFlexONE Loan Origination System provides a single system of record all mortgage and consumer loan types. All our portals use the same database as the LOS, giving you all the compliance and security without the pain of complex integration software. All LOS tools are readily available - real-time origination analysis, pre-qualification, application, image capture, fees, and PPE.

Our customers are closing 3 or more loans per user, per month. This accounts for 200% improvement without hiring additional employees.

Compliant

We constantly check updated loan data with our Product and Pricing Engine. Errors are identified as they happen in real-time. We match our calculations with a third-party during the preparation of the initial disclosure and closing package.

Scalable

MortgageFlexONE is designed to grow with your business. The system adapts to your lending needs as your company changes, becoming ever more efficient. We can scale performance to meet peak needs. If you have a busy month, we can quickly add power to match those needs, then decrease them when you are ready. No other provider can do that for you.

  • Compliance Support – Rules-based compliance monitoring prevents improper disclosures and loan movement
  • Workflow – Lender driven workflow automation tools
  • Product & Pricing – embedded PPE, adjustments and eligibility rules
  • Image Management – comprehensive image management; organized content eFolder

Consumer Portal

The Consumer portal is designed to support lenders, so your branding is fully integrated from day one, giving your customers a look and feel they can trust. Loan applications and inquiries flow directly to the loan origination system. Few things are as difficult as waiting for loan approval, but MortgageFlexONE Consumer gives a borrower confidence that you are working on their loan. Borrowers can start an application, stop, save it, return to it later, and check their status as often as they like. We’ve even built in detailed mortgage calculators to help them make the right decision.

TPO Portal

The MortgageFlexONE TPO portal makes it faster and easier than ever to work together in real time for unprecedented levels of visibility and flexibility. Remote employees can price, lock and get an AU recommendation all at once. They can search and locate your latest products, rates, and data as well as set the necessary conditions and controls. The TPO portal can also be branded with your look and feel.

Branch Portal

The MortgageFlexOne Branch portal lets anyone at any location input leads, take loan applications, and check status. The simplified user interface allows any designated branch personnel to start a loan application, assign a loan officer, and get the ball rolling for your customer - with minimal training. The MortgageFlexONE Branch portal can be branded using your logo along with the look and feel your employees are familiar with.

Servicing System

The MortgageFlex Servicing Portal extends your customer service support to 24/7. The portal provides customer friendly self-service support for the most common requests made to your customer service department. The information is presented in real time and offers convenient access on multiple devices including web browsers, tablets and phones. Customer satisfaction is improved while significantly reducing call volume to customer service.
