Our Story

MortgageFlex Systems was founded in 1980 for one reason - to simplify mortgage lending. After 36 years, we're still focused on the mortgage industry and dedicated to providing lenders with smart software solutions.

To accomplish our mission, our team is comprised of mortgage and software development experts. Most of our staff has well over ten years of experience, with many edging to the twenty year mark. We cultivate a strong cross-learning culture with shared responsibilities and we're dedicated to our customer's success.
