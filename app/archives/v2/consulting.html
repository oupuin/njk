MortgageFlex Consulting Services


Direct client support is a major part of the MortgageFlex Systems experience. Whether at the beginning stages of an implementation, or already live, you will receive support from a single request. Our goal is to acknowledge all requests immediately and then we work with the customer to prioritize the request and correct the problem. We provide a LIVE person to talk with - you don't have to wait 24 hours or so for a callback. We're here to help!

Business Process Analysis

Our staff engages with the prospective client to understand their particular business process and uses that knowledge break down the functions into components supportable with the existing MSI technology. The analyst provides a write up describing a best practices marriage of the clients process requirements and the MSI systems.

Roadmap Development

Assist the client by understanding their goals and objectives and aligning them with the MSI product enhancement roadmap.

Project Management

Working with the client, the MSI Project Manager will coordinate the efforts of the cleints staff and the MSI analysts to install the most efficient and effective system possible, configured specifically for the client.

Subject Matter Experts

Not just in the use of our product but as our Consulting staff is made up of experienced industry professionals with deep understanding of the lending practice and the regulations guiding the processes and the deployment of the system.

Business Process Improvement

Business process improvement More than the Business Process Analyst, the Analysts and consultants will work with the BPA results and the operational goals of the organization and with the requisite rules and regulations plus their experience working with other lenders to construct a highly efficient and effective system deployment.

Implementation Support

The analysts will collect all the pertinent lender data (products, fees, general parameters, vendors, employees, security settings, employee roles, etc…) and will use this information to configure the system specifically for the client.

Testing

The Consultants will work with the client to test and measure the effectiveness of their new system looking for effeciencies where possible.

Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting When something does not go to plan, the Consultant will use their extensive experience to help troubleshoot any issue that arises through the implementation process and after go-live.

User Case Studies

The Consultant will bring to the engagement the knowledge gained over time to help the client solve complex operational challenges.

Tailoring

Tailoring The MSI software comes with a powerful set of tools for tailoring the system to make it effective for the unique needs of each client. From the application of security settings, and custom menu creation to the deployment of DynaFields, DynaScreens and screen scripts, the MSI toolkit will insure you have leading edge technology solution tailored specifically for your unique needs.
