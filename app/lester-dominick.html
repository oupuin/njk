Lester Dominick


Lester Dominick is the founder and owner of MortgageFlex Systems.

In 1980, while working as a consultant at Arthur Young & Company (now Ernst & Young), he identified the need for information technology consulting services specifically for mortgage lending. Lester founded MortgageFlex Systems and focused solely on the mortgage lending industry, introducing the first microcomputer based loan origination system in 1984.

He continues to lead the company as CEO and chief product architect.

Recognized in 2008 as an "Industry Pioneer" by Mortgage Banking Magazine, Lester is widely acknowledged as an industry expert and MortgageFlex Systems continues to receive trade accolades.

Lester holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Management Science from Duke University and a MBA in Management Information Systems and Finance from the University of Southern California.
