MortgageFlex Support


Direct client support is a major part of the MortgageFlex Systems experience. Whether at the beginning stages of an implementation, or already live, you will receive support from a single request. Our goal is to acknowledge all requests immediately and then we work with the customer to prioritize the request and correct the problem. We provide a LIVE person to talk with - you don't have to wait 24 hours or so for a callback. We're here to help!

The technical support staff is made up of a diverse group of people each with a unique skill set. The technical services department consists of Microsoft Certified Professionals, Citrix Certified Administrators, and Network Engineers with over 25 years of experience planning, developing, implementing, maintaining, and managing data center environments, physical and virtual infrastructures. Technical services management has advanced degrees in information technology, business administration, and project management to offer the customer an unequaled support experience.

  • Phone Support - 8:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M. (EST)
  • Programming Support - 8:00 A.M. - 7:00 P.M. (M-Th), 8:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M. (F)
  • 24X7 hosted support - hosted support never closes
  • LinkedIn customer groups that allow customers to exchange ideas
  • Secure customer web-site for case submission
